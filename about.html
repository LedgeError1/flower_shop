About Rustavi Blooms

Rustavi Blooms is more than just a flower shop — we're a community rooted in passion, nature, and beauty. Located in the heart of Rustavi, our team of florists is dedicated to creating moments of joy through fresh, handcrafted floral arrangements. Whether it’s a birthday, wedding, or a simple “thinking of you,” we believe every bloom can make life a little brighter.

How We Farm Our Flowers

At Rustavi Blooms, our flowers are grown with care and purpose. We partner with local growers who share our commitment to sustainability and quality. Each flower is nurtured from seed to stem using eco-friendly methods, ensuring vibrant colors, long-lasting freshness, and minimal environmental impact. From field to bouquet, every step is done with love and respect for the earth.
